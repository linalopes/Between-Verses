
## Side Arms Pose Guide

### What the pose should look like:
1. **Stand upright** with feet shoulder-width apart
2. **Raise both elbows** to shoulder level (like you're going to flex your biceps)
3. **Point your hands/wrists upward** - like you're celebrating a victory
4. **Keep elbows wide** - away from your body to the sides
5. **Both arms should be symmetric** - same position on both sides
